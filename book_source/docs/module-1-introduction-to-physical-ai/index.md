# Module 1: Introduction to Physical AI

Welcome to Module 1 of "Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics"! This module lays the groundwork for understanding the convergence of artificial intelligence with physical systems, focusing on how AI can interact with and understand the real world through embodied agents.

We will explore the fundamental concepts that define Physical AI, delve into the exciting and rapidly evolving landscape of humanoid robotics, and examine the critical role of various sensor systems that enable these robots to perceive their environment.
